## Who Is Zelt, Exactly?

Zelt Technologies Limited (operating under ZELT Global Ventures FZC LLC) is a UAE-based hosting company with a pretty wide product catalog — shared hosting, cloud SSD hosting, VPS, dedicated servers, Windows hosting, e-commerce hosting, reseller plans, and even DMCA-ignored (offshore bulletproof) hosting. They serve over 20,000 customers and position themselves squarely for small-to-medium businesses that want solid performance without having to call someone in California at 3am.

Their infrastructure runs on enterprise-grade HP servers with RAID-10 SSD storage, LiteSpeed web servers, and Cloudflare CDN integration baked in. The whole stack is DDoS-protected at Layer 7 — which they call "military-grade," a bit dramatic, but the underlying tech is real.

One thing worth noting: 95% of support tickets, they claim, get a response in under 10 minutes. That's a bold number. User feedback on their site (including from a Diageo technician and a professional photographer who's been with them since 2006) seems to back that up pretty consistently.

## Who Should Use Zelt?

**Great fit if you:**
- Are based in the UAE/Middle East and want local servers with regional support
- Run a small-to-medium business site and want cPanel without fussing over server management
- Need DMCA-ignored or offshore hosting (Zelt is one of the few providers that openly offers this from Dubai)

- Are a web developer or agency who wants reseller capabilities with good margins

**Might not be the best fit if:**
- You need servers in Europe or North America specifically (latency from those regions could be higher)
- You're looking for the absolute cheapest shared hosting (providers like some US-based options go lower on intro pricing, but often without the included features)
